Reporting and Analytics in Salesforce Vlocity OM

Discover how to leverage reporting and analytics features in Salesforce Vlocity OM to gain valuable insights into your order management processes.

Introduction

Effective reporting and analytics are crucial for understanding your order management performance and making data-driven decisions. This guide will introduce you to the reporting and analytics capabilities available in Salesforce Vlocity OM.

Key Reporting and Analytics Areas

  • Standard Reports: Pre-built reports available out-of-the-box.
  • Custom Reports: Creating tailored reports to meet specific business needs.
  • Dashboards: Building visual representations of key metrics and KPIs.
  • Einstein Analytics: Utilizing AI-powered analytics for deeper insights.
  • Report Types: Understanding and creating custom report types for complex reporting needs.

Hands-On Lab: Creating OM Reports and Dashboards

This lab will guide you through the process of creating reports and dashboards in Salesforce Vlocity OM.

Lab Steps

  1. Log into your Salesforce org with Vlocity OM installed.
  2. Create a custom report to track order fulfillment times.
  3. Build a dashboard to visualize key order management metrics.
  4. Set up scheduled report runs and email notifications for stakeholders.
  5. Explore Einstein Analytics features for predictive insights on order trends.
  6. Create a custom report type for analyzing complex order relationships.

Best Practices for OM Reporting and Analytics

  • Clearly define your reporting objectives before building reports or dashboards.
  • Use consistent naming conventions for reports and dashboards to improve discoverability.
  • Leverage report folders to organize reports by function or department.
  • Regularly review and update your reports to ensure they remain relevant and accurate.
  • Provide training to end-users on how to interpret and act on the insights provided.

Advanced Reporting and Analytics Topics

  • Cross-Object Reporting: Creating reports that span multiple objects for comprehensive insights.
  • Predictive Analytics: Using machine learning models to forecast order trends and outcomes.
  • External Data Integration: Incorporating data from external sources for more comprehensive reporting.
  • Custom Visualizations: Developing custom chart types and visualizations for unique data representations.
