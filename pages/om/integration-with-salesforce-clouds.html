Integration with Salesforce Clouds in Vlocity OM

Discover how Salesforce Vlocity Order Management integrates with other Salesforce cloud offerings to create a unified business platform.

Introduction

Salesforce Vlocity OM is designed to work seamlessly with other Salesforce cloud products. Understanding these integrations can help you leverage the full power of the Salesforce ecosystem.

Key Integration Points

  • Sales Cloud: Connecting order management with opportunity and account management.
  • Service Cloud: Linking orders to customer service and support processes.
  • Marketing Cloud: Utilizing order data for targeted marketing campaigns.
  • Commerce Cloud: Integrating e-commerce operations with order management.
  • Einstein Analytics: Leveraging AI-powered insights across the order lifecycle.

Hands-On Lab: Exploring Cloud Integrations

This lab will guide you through the process of setting up and utilizing integrations between Vlocity OM and other Salesforce clouds.

Lab Steps

  1. Log into your Salesforce org with Vlocity OM and other cloud products installed.
  2. Set up a workflow to create an order from a closed-won opportunity in Sales Cloud.
  3. Configure Service Cloud to display relevant order information in the customer service console.
  4. Create a Marketing Cloud journey that triggers based on specific order events.
  5. Integrate Commerce Cloud product catalog with Vlocity OM for consistent pricing and availability.
  6. Build an Einstein Analytics dashboard that combines data from OM and other clouds for comprehensive insights.

Best Practices for Cloud Integration

  • Develop a holistic view of your business processes across all clouds before implementing integrations.
  • Ensure data consistency and integrity across integrated cloud products.
  • Implement proper security measures to protect sensitive data shared between clouds.
  • Regularly review and optimize your integrations to ensure they continue to meet business needs.
  • Provide comprehensive training to users on how to work with integrated cloud systems.

Advanced Integration Topics

  • Mulesoft Integration: Utilizing Mulesoft for advanced integration scenarios.
  • Multi-Org Strategies: Managing integrations in complex, multi-org Salesforce environments.
  • Integration Monitoring and Maintenance: Best practices for ongoing management of cloud integrations.
