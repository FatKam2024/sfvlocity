CPQ Data Model in Salesforce Vlocity

Understand the CPQ data model within Salesforce Vlocity, including key objects, relationships, and best practices for data management.

Introduction

The CPQ data model is the backbone of your configuration, pricing, and quoting processes. This guide will provide an in-depth look at the key components of the CPQ data model in Salesforce Vlocity.

Key Components

  • Products: Managing product data and attributes.
  • Price Books: Configuring price books and associated pricing data.
  • Quotes: Creating and managing quote records.
  • Contracts: Linking quotes to contracts and managing contract data.
  • Orders: Converting quotes into orders and managing order data.

Hands-On Lab: Data Model Configuration

This lab will guide you through configuring the CPQ data model in Salesforce Vlocity.

Lab Steps

  1. Log into your Salesforce org with Vlocity CPQ installed.
  2. Set up product data and attributes within the CPQ data model.
  3. Configure price books and link them to relevant products.
  4. Create a sample quote and manage its data within the model.
  5. Convert the quote into an order and ensure data integrity throughout the process.

Best Practices for Data Model Management

  • Regularly review and update product data to ensure accuracy.
  • Maintain consistency in pricing data across all price books.
  • Ensure that all quotes are accurately linked to contracts and orders.
  • Leverage data validation rules to maintain data integrity.
  • Document all changes to the CPQ data model for future reference and auditing.
