Product Catalog Management in Salesforce Vlocity

Learn how to manage product catalogs efficiently within Salesforce Vlocity, covering key features, best practices, and configuration steps.

Introduction

Effective product catalog management is essential for delivering the right products and services to your customers. This guide will help you optimize product catalog management in Salesforce Vlocity.

Key Topics

  • Catalog Structure: Organizing products and services within the catalog.
  • Product Bundling: Creating and managing product bundles.
  • Pricing Models: Configuring pricing models for different products.
  • Catalog Synchronization: Syncing product catalogs across multiple channels.
  • Catalog Updates: Managing updates and version control.

Hands-On Lab: Product Catalog Configuration

This lab will guide you through the configuration of a product catalog within Salesforce Vlocity.

Lab Steps

  1. Log into your Salesforce org with Vlocity CPQ installed.
  2. Set up a new product catalog with sample products and services.
  3. Create product bundles and configure pricing models.
  4. Sync the catalog with multiple sales channels.
  5. Manage catalog updates and ensure version control.

Best Practices for Product Catalog Management

  • Regularly review and update product catalogs to ensure accuracy.
  • Use product bundling to offer value-added services to customers.
  • Ensure pricing models are consistent and competitive.
  • Maintain version control to track changes in the product catalog.
  • Test catalog updates thoroughly before going live.
