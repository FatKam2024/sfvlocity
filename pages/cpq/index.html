CPQ - Configure, Price, Quote

Welcome to the CPQ course. This module is designed to equip you with the essential skills to configure complex product offerings, determine pricing strategies, and generate accurate quotes using Salesforce Vlocity CPQ.

Introduction to CPQ

Configure, Price, Quote (CPQ) is a powerful sales tool that enables organizations to quickly and accurately generate complex quotes for products and services. Salesforce Vlocity CPQ enhances the standard Salesforce CPQ functionality with industry-specific features and advanced capabilities.

Key Benefits of CPQ:

  • Streamlined quote-to-cash process
  • Increased quote accuracy and reduced errors
  • Faster quote generation and approval times
  • Improved sales productivity and efficiency
  • Enhanced customer experience

Core Components of Salesforce Vlocity CPQ:

  1. Product Catalog Management
  2. Pricing and Discounting Engines
  3. Quote Generation and Management
  4. Guided Selling
  5. Contract Management
  6. Order Management Integration

In this course, you'll learn how to leverage these components to optimize your sales processes and drive business growth.
