Quoting Process & CPQ Quote Tutorial in Salesforce Vlocity

This guide covers the entire quoting process in Salesforce Vlocity, including configuration, automation, and best practices for generating accurate quotes. It also includes a tutorial on setting up and managing quotes within the CPQ module.

Introduction

Quoting is a critical component of the sales process, enabling businesses to generate accurate and timely quotes for customers. This document combines the quoting process overview with a detailed tutorial on managing quotes within Salesforce Vlocity CPQ.

Key Topics

  • Quote Setup: Basic setup and configuration of quotes in CPQ.
  • Quote Templates: Creating and managing standardized templates.
  • Pricing Rules: Implementing pricing rules to ensure accuracy.
  • Discounting: Setting up discounting options and approval workflows.
  • Quote Automation: Automating the quote generation process.
  • Quote Management: Tracking and managing quote statuses and revisions.
  • Advanced Quote Features: Exploring advanced features like custom approvals and dynamic pricing.

Hands-On Lab: Quoting Process & CPQ Quote Configuration

This lab will guide you through configuring and managing quotes in Salesforce Vlocity CPQ.

Lab Steps

  1. Log into your Salesforce org with Vlocity CPQ installed.
  2. Create a new quote template and configure pricing rules.
  3. Set up discounting options with approval workflows.
  4. Automate the quote generation process for faster turnaround.
  5. Manage and track the status of generated quotes.
  6. Explore advanced quote features, such as dynamic pricing and custom approvals.

Best Practices for Quoting & CPQ Quote Management

  • Regularly review and update quote templates to ensure they meet business needs.
  • Ensure pricing rules are consistently applied across all quotes.
  • Leverage automation to reduce manual errors and speed up the quoting process.
  • Document all quoting processes for compliance and auditing purposes.
  • Monitor the performance of the quoting process and make adjustments as needed.
