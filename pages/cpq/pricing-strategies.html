Pricing Strategies & Advanced Pricing in Salesforce Vlocity

This guide explores both fundamental and advanced pricing strategies within Salesforce Vlocity, providing you with the tools to implement dynamic and effective pricing models.

Introduction

Pricing is a critical aspect of business strategy. This document combines foundational pricing strategies with advanced techniques to help you maximize revenue and stay competitive using Salesforce Vlocity.

Key Pricing Strategies

  • Cost-Plus Pricing: Adding a markup to the cost of goods sold.
  • Value-Based Pricing: Setting prices based on perceived value to the customer.
  • Dynamic Pricing: Adjusting prices in real-time based on market conditions.
  • Tiered Pricing: Offering different pricing tiers based on customer segments.
  • Freemium Models: Providing a basic product for free with premium options.

Advanced Pricing Techniques

  • Price Books: Configuring and managing complex price books.
  • Discount Schedules: Creating and applying tiered discounts.
  • Pricing Rules: Setting up conditional pricing rules based on business logic.
  • Dynamic Pricing Models: Implementing real-time pricing adjustments based on various factors.
  • Revenue Optimization: Strategies to optimize pricing for maximum revenue.

Hands-On Lab: Implementing Pricing Strategies & Advanced Pricing

This lab will guide you through setting up and implementing both basic and advanced pricing strategies in Salesforce Vlocity.

Lab Steps

  1. Log into your Salesforce org with Vlocity CPQ installed.
  2. Configure cost-plus pricing for a specific product.
  3. Set up value-based pricing based on customer segments.
  4. Implement dynamic pricing for a time-sensitive promotion.
  5. Create and manage a complex price book.
  6. Set up a discount schedule with multiple tiers based on purchase volume.
  7. Test the pricing strategies in different scenarios to ensure effectiveness.

Best Practices for Pricing Strategy Implementation

  • Regularly review and adjust pricing strategies to align with market conditions.
  • Ensure pricing models are clear and easily understood by customers.
  • Leverage data analytics to refine and optimize pricing strategies.
  • Test pricing strategies in a sandbox environment before going live.
  • Monitor the performance of pricing strategies and make adjustments as needed.
