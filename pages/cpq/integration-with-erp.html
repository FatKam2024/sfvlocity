Best Practices for ERP Integration

  • Start with a clear integration strategy that aligns with business goals.
  • Ensure data mapping is accurate to prevent data discrepancies between systems.
  • Test integrations thoroughly in a sandbox environment before deploying to production.
  • Document integration processes for future reference and troubleshooting.
  • Monitor and maintain integrations regularly to ensure ongoing effectiveness.
