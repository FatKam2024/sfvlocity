Reporting & Analytics in Salesforce Vlocity

Learn how to utilize reporting and analytics tools in Salesforce Vlocity to gain insights into your business performance and make data-driven decisions.

Introduction

Reporting and analytics are crucial for monitoring business performance and identifying trends. This guide will help you harness the power of Salesforce Vlocity’s reporting tools to gain actionable insights.

Key Reporting & Analytics Tools

  • Reports: Creating and customizing reports to analyze data.
  • Dashboards: Visualizing key metrics through interactive dashboards.
  • Einstein Analytics: Leveraging AI-driven insights for deeper analysis.
  • Custom Reporting: Building custom reports to meet specific business needs.
  • Data Export: Exporting data for further analysis in external tools.

Hands-On Lab: Creating Reports & Dashboards

This lab will guide you through creating and customizing reports and dashboards in Salesforce Vlocity.

Lab Steps

  1. Log into your Salesforce org with Vlocity installed.
  2. Create a basic report to analyze sales performance.
  3. Customize the report to include additional data fields.
  4. Set up a dashboard to visualize key metrics from the report.
  5. Explore advanced analytics using Salesforce Einstein.

Best Practices for Reporting & Analytics

  • Regularly review and update reports to ensure they reflect current business needs.
  • Use dashboards to monitor key metrics at a glance and identify trends.
  • Leverage AI tools like Einstein Analytics for deeper insights and predictions.
  • Document reporting processes and configurations for future reference.
  • Export data as needed for additional analysis in external tools like Excel or Tableau.
