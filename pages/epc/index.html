Introduction to EPC

The Enterprise Product Catalog (EPC) is a critical component in Salesforce Vlocity, enabling businesses to manage complex product definitions, pricing, rules, and relationships efficiently. EPC serves as the foundation for Configure, Price, Quote (CPQ) and Order Management (OM) processes, ensuring consistent and accurate product information across the entire quote-to-cash cycle.

Key Benefits of EPC

  • Centralized product management for improved consistency and accuracy
  • Flexible product modeling to support complex offerings and bundles
  • Dynamic pricing and discounting capabilities
  • Streamlined catalog management for faster time-to-market
  • Enhanced customer experience through personalized product offerings
