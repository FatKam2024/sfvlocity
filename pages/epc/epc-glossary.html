EPC Glossary

This glossary provides definitions for common terms used in Salesforce Vlocity Enterprise Product Catalog (EPC).

Attribute
A characteristic or feature of a product that can be used to define its properties or variations.
Catalog
A collection of products or services organized for easy navigation and selection.
Product Model
A template that defines the structure, attributes, and behavior of a product or service.
Relationship
A connection between different products or components within the product catalog, such as parent-child or cross-sell relationships.
Pricing Rule
A set of conditions and actions that determine how prices are calculated for products or services.
Bundle
A group of related products or services sold together as a package.
Constraint Rule
A rule that defines limitations or requirements for product configurations or combinations.
Dynamic Catalog
A catalog that adjusts its content based on various factors such as user profile, location, or time.
Attribute-Based Pricing
A pricing method where the final price of a product is determined by its specific attribute values.
Version Control
The management of changes to product models, catalogs, and pricing rules over time.
Product Hierarchy
The organization of products into a tree-like structure, showing relationships between parent and child products.
Compatibility Rule
A rule that defines which products or options can be combined or are mutually exclusive.
Tiered Pricing
A pricing strategy where the price per unit changes as the quantity purchased increases.
Product Lifecycle Management
The process of managing a product's entire lifecycle from conception, through design and manufacture, to service and disposal.
Configurator
A tool that allows users to customize products by selecting various options and attributes.
Promotion
A marketing offer that provides a discount or added value to encourage the purchase of specific products or services.
