Catalog Management

Catalog management in EPC allows you to organize products, offers, and pricing into logical groups that align with your business strategy.

Creating and Managing Catalogs

Learn how to create catalogs in Salesforce Vlocity, including organizing products and setting up rules for catalog visibility and access.

Steps to Create a Catalog

  1. Create a new catalog in the EPC module.
  2. Add products and offers to the catalog, organizing them based on business requirements.
  3. Set up rules for who can view and access the catalog.
  4. Test the catalog setup in a sandbox environment.
