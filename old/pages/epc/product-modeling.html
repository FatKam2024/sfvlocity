Product Modeling

In this section, we explore how to model products in Salesforce Vlocity's EPC, covering the key concepts, attributes, and relationships.

Understanding Product Models

Product models define the structure and components of a product, allowing for customization and scalability. Each model can include multiple attributes, such as size, color, and material, and can be linked to other models through relationships.

Key Components of Product Modeling

  • Attributes: Define the characteristics of the product.
  • Relationships: Establish connections between different product components.
  • Versioning: Manage different versions of a product model to track changes over time.

Lab: Creating a Product Model

  1. Navigate to the EPC module in Salesforce.
  2. Create a new product model and define its attributes.
  3. Link the product model to other related models.
  4. Test the product model by simulating customer scenarios.
