Best Practices

  • Modular Design: Build product models and catalogs in a modular way to allow for flexibility and scalability.
  • Regular Updates: Keep your product models and pricing rules updated to reflect market changes and customer feedback.
  • Testing: Regularly test your catalog setup in a sandbox environment to catch potential issues before they impact customers.
