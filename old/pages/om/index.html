OM - Order Management

Welcome to the OM course. In this module, you will master the skills needed to efficiently capture, process, and fulfill orders using Salesforce Vlocity Order Management.

Introduction to Order Management

Salesforce Vlocity Order Management (OM) enables businesses to streamline the order lifecycle from capture to fulfillment. This is essential for industries such as telecommunications, retail, and utilities, where accurate and efficient order processing is critical to customer satisfaction and business success.

Key Concepts

  • Order Capture: Collect orders from various channels including online, in-store, and customer service interactions.
  • Order Processing: Automate workflows to ensure orders are processed efficiently and accurately, with minimal manual intervention.
  • Order Fulfillment: Manage the fulfillment process to ensure timely and accurate delivery of products or services.
  • Integration: Connect OM with other systems like ERP, CRM, and billing to maintain data consistency and streamline operations.

Hands-On Labs & Exercises

Engage in practical labs that will help you manage the entire order lifecycle, from capture to fulfillment, using real-world scenarios.

Lab 1: Capturing Orders from Multiple Channels

  1. Set up order capture processes from various channels, including online stores, physical retail locations, and call centers.
  2. Ensure the order data is standardized and synchronized across all channels.
  3. Test the order capture process by simulating orders from different sources.

Lab 2: Automating Order Processing

  1. Define workflows for processing orders based on criteria such as priority, inventory availability, and customer segmentation.
  2. Implement automation to reduce manual steps, ensuring efficiency and accuracy in order processing.
  3. Monitor the workflow to identify and resolve any bottlenecks or issues.

Lab 3: Managing Order Fulfillment

  1. Set up the fulfillment process, including picking, packing, and shipping orders.
  2. Integrate with third-party logistics providers to streamline the shipping process.
  3. Test the fulfillment workflow to ensure orders are delivered accurately and on time.
