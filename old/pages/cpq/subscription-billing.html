Subscription Billing

Learn how to manage subscription billing within Salesforce CPQ, including setting up recurring billing, managing renewals, and integrating with external billing systems.

Setting Up Subscription Billing

Subscription billing allows you to set up recurring charges for products and services. This is particularly useful for businesses offering software-as-a-service (SaaS) or other subscription-based products.

Steps to Implement Subscription Billing

  1. Define subscription products and their billing cycles in Salesforce CPQ.
  2. Set up automated billing schedules that trigger charges at the appropriate intervals.
  3. Integrate with external billing systems to handle payments and invoices.

Managing Renewals

Automate the renewal process to ensure that subscriptions are extended without interruption. This includes setting up notifications for upcoming renewals and automating the renewal contract generation.
