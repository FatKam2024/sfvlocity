Advanced Pricing Strategies

This page explores advanced pricing strategies that you can implement using Salesforce Vlocity CPQ.

Dynamic Pricing

Dynamic pricing adjusts prices in real-time based on various factors such as demand, customer segment, and market conditions. Learn how to implement dynamic pricing using Salesforce rules and triggers.

Steps to Implement Dynamic Pricing

  1. Define the criteria for price adjustments, such as customer loyalty, purchase history, or market demand.
  2. Set up automated rules in Salesforce CPQ to adjust pricing based on the defined criteria.
  3. Test the dynamic pricing setup in various scenarios to ensure accuracy and effectiveness.

Bundle Pricing

Bundle pricing involves offering multiple products or services together at a discounted rate. This strategy can increase sales and customer satisfaction.

Steps to Implement Bundle Pricing

  1. Identify products or services that can be bundled together based on customer needs or market trends.
  2. Create pricing rules in Salesforce CPQ to apply discounts when products are purchased as a bundle.
  3. Test the bundle pricing configuration to ensure it correctly applies discounts and meets business objectives.

Volume Discounts

Volume discounts incentivize customers to purchase more by offering discounts based on the quantity purchased. This section covers how to set up and manage volume discounts in Salesforce CPQ.

Steps to Implement Volume Discounts

  1. Define the discount tiers based on the volume of products purchased.
  2. Set up automated discount rules in Salesforce CPQ to apply the appropriate discount based on purchase volume.
  3. Monitor and adjust the discount strategy based on sales performance and customer feedback.
