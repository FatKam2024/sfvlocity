Guided Selling

Guided selling helps sales teams navigate complex product configurations and ensures they offer the most suitable products to customers.

Implementing Guided Selling in CPQ

Guided selling in Salesforce CPQ involves creating a series of prompts and rules that guide sales representatives through the product selection process. This ensures that the most appropriate products and options are offered to the customer, based on their needs and preferences.

Steps to Implement Guided Selling

  1. Create a decision tree that maps out the product selection process.
  2. Define rules and prompts in Salesforce CPQ that guide the sales representative through this tree.
  3. Test the guided selling process with different customer scenarios to ensure it works as expected.

Advanced Techniques

Learn advanced techniques such as integrating guided selling with AI-powered recommendations or using customer data to dynamically adjust the guided selling process.
