Contract Management

Contract management in CPQ automates the creation, approval, and management of contracts linked to quotes and sales orders.

Automating Contract Creation

Automate the generation of contracts based on the products and services included in a quote. This ensures consistency and reduces the risk of errors.

Steps to Set Up Contract Management

  1. Define contract templates in Salesforce CPQ.
  2. Link these templates to specific products and services within your product catalog.
  3. Automate the approval process to ensure contracts are reviewed and signed off before finalizing the sale.

Advanced Contract Management

Explore advanced features such as contract amendments, renewals, and integration with external contract management systems.
